How Grabbit Works

Revolutionary location-based notifications that turn everyday errands into community-building opportunities.

Location-Based Notifications

When someone walks into a store, they get a ping on their phone if another group member needs something from there, along with a prepayment to reduce financial friction. It's not traditional delivery.

Community First

Perfect for tight-knit communities like dorms, roommates, apartment buildings, and co-ops. Build stronger relationships while helping each other with daily needs.

Effortless Shopping

People already at the store grab what others need, dropping it off on their own way home. No extra trips, no delivery fees, just community cooperation.

Time Saving

Skip the extra trips for sticky notes, snacks, or forgotten items. Pool requests so one person's trip benefits the entire community.

Social Currency

Helpers earn social credit and in-app currency (eventually convertible to money). Requesters pay nothing extra while building community goodwill.

Smart Notifications

Only get notified for stores you're actually visiting. No spam, no checking lists in advance - just grab and go when it's convenient.
